# Simplicity Growth Marketing
## Agency Context & Internal AI Agent System

---

## Who We Are

Simplicity Growth Marketing is a **Growth Marketing and Advertising agency** focused on helping **owner‑led local businesses and brands** grow with clarity, consistency, and scalability.

Simplicity combines:
- deep market and brand research
- growth strategy
- paid advertising campaigns
- strategic content systems
- data‑driven creative direction
- automation and artificial intelligence

Simplicity does not sell isolated deliverables.
Simplicity designs and operates **growth systems**.
