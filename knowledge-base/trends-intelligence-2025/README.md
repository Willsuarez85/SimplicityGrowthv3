## Key Findings Summary

### Audience Behavior
- **49%** of Hispanic adults use TikTok (highest demographic penetration)
- **58%** of Latinos use Instagram
- **73%** of Latino audiences use YouTube
- **89%** of Hispanic audiences engage more with Spanish-language content
- **Mobile-first:** 92-94% of consumption on mobile devices

### Content Performance
- **First 1-3 seconds:** Critical hook window across all platforms
- **Educational content:** Dominates when delivered with authentic energy
- **Bilingual content:** Shows 34% higher reach than English-only
- **Behind-the-scenes:** Outperforms polished corporate content
- **AI tool tutorials:** High demand, low supply (especially Spanish)

### Platform Priorities
- **TikTok:** Comment engagement weighted heavier, original sound gaining favor
- **Instagram:** Save rate primary signal, de-emphasizing hashtags
- **YouTube:** Watch time still king, Shorts-to-long-form cross-promotion beneficial

### Cultural Signals
- Family-oriented messaging resonates strongly
- Community over competition themes drive engagement
- Immigrant entrepreneurship stories build connection
- Practical education preferred over theory
