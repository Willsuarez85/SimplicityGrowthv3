## Content Quality Checklist

Before any content goes to production, verify:

### Strategic Alignment
- [ ] Fits one of three pillars (AGENTES/SISTEMAS/NEGOCIO)
- [ ] Matches audience stage (Awareness/Interest/Consideration/Decision)
- [ ] Connects to content calendar theme
- [ ] Supports brand positioning

### Voice & Tone
- [ ] Clara - No unnecessary jargon
- [ ] Directa - Gets to the point
- [ ] Tranquila - Confident, not arrogant
- [ ] Did√°ctica - Uses examples and analogies
- [ ] Sin exageraciones - No hype words

### Production Readiness
- [ ] Hook is scroll-stopping
- [ ] One clear message per piece
- [ ] CTA is actionable (not aggressive)
- [ ] Visual direction is clear
- [ ] All required assets identified

### The Golden Filter
> **"Si algo no se puede implementar, no pertenece a AIPreneur."**

- [ ] Is it actionable?
- [ ] Does it have clear steps?
- [ ] Does it lead to a tangible result?
