## Framework Overview

This document provides the structural framework for planning AIPreneur content on a weekly, monthly, and quarterly basis. It establishes the rhythm, distribution, and thematic organization that ensures consistent, strategic content production.

### Core Principles

1. **Pillar Balance** - Every week touches all three pillars (AGENTES, SISTEMAS, NEGOCIO)
2. **Platform Optimization** - Right content for right platform at right time
3. **Audience Journey** - Content progression from awareness to conversion
4. **Sustainable Rhythm** - Achievable cadence that can be maintained long-term
5. **Flexibility** - Room for trending topics and opportunistic content

## Monthly Theme Structure

### Monthly Theme Approach

Each month has a **Primary Theme** that unifies content while maintaining pillar diversity.

**Theme Selection Criteria:**
1. Relevant to current audience needs
2. Allows for all three pillars
3. Timely (seasonal, trending, or strategic)
4. Deep enough for month-long exploration

### Monthly Planning Template

```
MONTH: [Month Name]
THEME: [One central topic/concept]
PILLAR WEIGHT: AGENTES __% | SISTEMAS __% | NEGOCIO __%

Week 1: [Sub-theme 1 - Usually AGENTES-heavy]
Week 2: [Sub-theme 2 - Building toward SISTEMAS]
Week 3: [Sub-theme 3 - SISTEMAS depth]
Week 4: [Sub-theme 4 - NEGOCIO application + review]
```

### Sample Monthly Themes

| Month | Theme | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| January | "Tu Primer Agente" | Building blocks, first steps |

## Content Slot System

### Slot-Based Planning

Instead of assigning specific topics far in advance, use **content slots** that define what TYPE of content goes where:

### Weekly Slot Template

| Slot | Platform | Type | Pillar | Notes |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|
| **A1** | YT Long | Tutorial | AGENTES/SISTEMAS | Main teaching content |
| **A2** | YT Long | Case Study | NEGOCIO | Results-focused |
| **B1-B5** | YT Short | Concept Bite | Rotating | From long-form + unique |
| **C1-C4** | TikTok | Native | AGENTES | Platform-specific |
| **D1-D3** | LinkedIn | Professional | NEGOCIO/SISTEMAS | Business angle |
| **E1-E2** | Twitter | Thread | SISTEMAS | Deep breakdowns |

### Slot Flexibility Rules

1. **Fixed Slots** - YouTube Long (must be produced fresh)
2. **Derived Slots** - Shorts, Reels (can derive from Long)
3. **Flexible Slots** - LinkedIn, Twitter (can be created day-of)
4. **Trend Slots** - 20% of short-form reserved for trending topics

## Trending Content Integration

### Trend Response Framework

**When a relevant trend appears:**

1. **Evaluate Fit** (10 min)
   - Does it connect to our pillars?
   - Can we add unique value?
   - Is timing still good?

2. **Quick Production** (1-2 hours)
   - Use Quick Brief template
   - Prioritize speed over polish
   - Platform-native execution

3. **Slot Replacement**
   - Replace a flexible slot (not fixed)
   - Note what was bumped for rescheduling
   - Track trend performance

### Trend Slot Allocation

Reserve 20% of short-form slots for trending content:

| Weekly Slots | Planned | Trend Reserve |
|--------------|---------|---------------|
| YouTube Shorts (7) | 5-6 | 1-2 |
| TikTok (7) | 5-6 | 1-2 |
| Instagram Reels (5) | 4 | 1 |

## Content Batching Strategy

**Weekly Batching Rhythm:**

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|

### Batch Sizes

| Content Type | Batch Size | Production Time |
|--------------|------------|-----------------|
| YouTube Long | 1-2 per session | 3-4 hours |
| Shorts/Reels | 5-7 per session | 2-3 hours |
| LinkedIn Posts | 5-7 per session | 1-2 hours |
| Twitter Threads | 2-3 per session | 1 hour |

## Calendar Review Cadence

### Weekly Review (15 min, Friday)

- Did we hit all slots?
- What performed best?
- What needs adjustment?
- Next week ready?

### Monthly Review (1 hour, Last Friday)

- Monthly metrics review
- Top/bottom performing content
- Pillar balance check
- Next month theme finalization

### Quarterly Review (3 hours)

- Quarterly goals assessment
- Content strategy adjustments
- Platform performance analysis
- Next quarter planning
