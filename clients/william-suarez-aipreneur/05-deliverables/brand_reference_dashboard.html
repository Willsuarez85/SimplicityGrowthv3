Eye Color Brown
Gaze Direct, focused, intentional
Expression Neutral to confident
Core Message "I understand systems. I can teach you."

APPROVED EXPRESSIONS

Neutral Confident
Default. Relaxed face, slight intensity in eyes.
Thoughtful
Educational content. Slight furrow, contemplative.
Direct/Serious
Authority pieces. Strong eye contact.
Subtle Smile
Approachable content. Corners slightly raised.

EXPRESSIONS TO AVOID

Broad Smiles
Too casual for brand.
Surprised
Exaggerated expressions.
Confused
Undermines authority.
Laughing
Too playful/casual.
