## Expression Guidelines

### Approved Expressions

| Expression | Use Case | Description |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| **Neutral Confident** | Default, most content | Relaxed face, slight intensity in eyes |
| **Thoughtful** | Educational content | Slight furrow, contemplative |
| **Direct/Serious** | Authority pieces | Strong eye contact, firm jaw |
| **Subtle Smile** | Approachable content | Corners of mouth slightly raised, eyes engaged |

### Expressions to Avoid

- Broad, toothy smiles (too casual)
- Surprised or exaggerated expressions
- Confused or uncertain looks
- Overly stern or intimidating
- Laughing or playful

### The Core Expression

> **"I understand systems. I can teach you."**

Eyes: Focused, confident, knowing
Mouth: Neutral to very slight smile
Overall: Approachable authority

## Photography/Portrait Style

### Technical Specifications

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Treatment** | Black and white OR color with muted tones |
| **Background** | Neutral dark (black, dark gray gradient) |
| **Lighting** | Studio quality, dramatic but not harsh |
| **Framing** | Head and shoulders, centered or rule-of-thirds |
| **Focus** | Sharp on face, especially eyes |
| **Depth of Field** | Shallow (blurred background) |

### Mood and Atmosphere

> **"Pienso con claridad. Diseño sistemas. Puedo guiarte."**
> (I think clearly. I design systems. I can guide you.)

The portrait should communicate:
- **Authority** without arrogance
- **Expertise** without pretension
- **Approachability** without casualness
- **Focus** without tension

### Background Options

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Dark Gradient** | Black to dark gray, vignette | Primary, most content |
| **Pure Black** | Solid #000000 | Maximum drama |
| **Dark Textured** | Subtle concrete/stone texture | Premium feel |
| **Clean White** | Pure #FFFFFF | High contrast, light mode |

## Pose Guidelines

### Approved Poses

| Pose | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Direct Front** | Shoulders square, face to camera | Authority, thumbnails |
| **Slight Turn** | Body 15-30° turned, face to camera | Dynamic, engaging |
| **Three-Quarter** | Body turned, looking over shoulder | Variety, interest |
| **Leaning Forward** | Slight lean toward camera | Engaging, teaching |

### Body Language Cues

**DO:**
- Relaxed shoulders (not tense)
- Chin slightly raised (confident)
- Direct eye contact with camera
- Arms relaxed at sides or crossed (confident, not defensive)

**DON'T:**
- Slouching or hunched
- Arms akimbo (aggressive)
- Looking away from camera (disengaged)
- Leaning back (distant)
