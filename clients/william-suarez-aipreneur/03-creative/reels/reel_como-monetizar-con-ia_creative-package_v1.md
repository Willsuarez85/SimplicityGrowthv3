## Executive Summary

This reel dismantles the hype around AI monetization and presents the three legitimate paths to building income with AI. The creative approach uses William's signature "calm authority" tone, whiteboard-style graphics, and a structure that challenges common misconceptions before delivering actionable insight.

**Core Message:** Monetization is not about knowing AI tools. It is about building systems that generate value.

**Emotional Arc:** Curiosity (hook) > Recognition (problem) > Challenge (misconception) > Clarity (framework) > Motivation (CTA)

## Part 8: Production Notes for Editor

### Visual Identity Reminders
- **Color:** Monochromatic base (90-95% black/white/gray)
- **Accent:** Sage green (#87A96B) used sparingly for underlines, highlights
- **Typography:** Sans-serif, geometric, high legibility
- **Graphics:** Whiteboard/marker style, imperfect lines

### Pacing Guidelines
- Hook: Immediate impact, no wasted frames
- Educational sections: Allow breathing room for comprehension
- Signature phrase: Full 2-second hold, let it land
- CTA: Warm energy, not rushed

### Transition Style
- Clean cuts preferred over effects
- Quick zoom for emphasis moments only
- Avoid: Flashy transitions, excessive motion graphics
- Allow: Subtle push-ins, natural cuts

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] Audio clean, no background noise
- [ ] Text readable on mobile (test at 50% size)
- [ ] Brand colors accurate (check sage green hex)
- [ ] Whiteboard graphics match brand style
- [ ] Pacing allows for message comprehension
- [ ] CTA visible and clear
