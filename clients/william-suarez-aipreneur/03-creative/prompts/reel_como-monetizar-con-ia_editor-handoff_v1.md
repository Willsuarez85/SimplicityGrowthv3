## Audio/Music Direction

**Music Style:**
- Tempo: 90-110 BPM
- Style: Lo-fi electronic, minimal beats, no lyrics
- Vibe: Confident, modern, professional (think Apple keynote)

**Music Levels:**
| Section | Music | Voice |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Hook | 20% | 100% |

| Framework | 40% | 100% |
| Real Game | 35% | 100% |
| Signature Phrase | 15% (drops) | 100% |
