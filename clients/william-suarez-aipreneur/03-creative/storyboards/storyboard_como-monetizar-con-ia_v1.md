## Color & Style Quick Reference

```
PRIMARY (90-95%):
- Deep black: #000000
- Clean white: #FFFFFF
- Neutral gray: #4A4A4A

ACCENT (5-8%):
- Sage green: #87A96B
- Use for: underlines, highlights, active states

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Sans-serif, geometric
- High legibility
- Weights: Regular, Medium, Bold

GRAPHICS:
- Whiteboard/marker style
- Imperfect (human) lines
- Monochromatic only
```
