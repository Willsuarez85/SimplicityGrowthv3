## Text Overlay Strategy

### Why Text Matters

- 85% de Reels/TikToks se ven sin audio inicialmente
- Text = accessibility (multitasking viewers)
- Reinforcement (visual + audio = better retention)
- SEO (algoritmo lee text para categorizaci√≥n)

### Design Guidelines

**Typography:**
- Font: Bold, sans-serif (Anton, Bebas Neue, Montserrat)
- Size: Large (25-30% de screen height para key text)
- Color: White con black outline (universal legibility)
- OR: Brand color con high contrast

**Animation:**
- Fade in/out (simple, non-distracting)
- Typewriter effect (para dramatic reveals)
- Pop-in con scale (para emphasis)
- NO: Excessive spinning, flashing (distracting)

**Placement:**
- Top third: Primary messaging
- Center: Rare (covers action)
- Bottom third: Steps, details
- Consistent across content (branding)

### Text Overlay Patterns

**Pattern 1: Step-by-Step**

### Transitions

**Effective Transitions:**
- Wipe (left, right, up, down)
- Flash/whip cut (quick energy)

- Dissolve (smooth, less jarring)

**Transition Timing:**
- Every scene/concept change
- Every 5-8 segundos para variety
- Align con music beat (satisfying sync)

**Avoid:**
- Excessive spinning, 3D rotations
- Slow fades (waste of seconds)
- Inconsistent style (choose 2-3 y stick)

### Testing Framework

**Test Variables:**

1. **Hook Variations**
   - Question vs statement vs number
   - Visual vs text-based
   - Track which drives higher completion

2. **Length Testing**
   - 15s vs 25s vs 40s
   - Find sweet spot for your niche
   - Technical content may need longer

3. **Music Testing**
   - Trending sound vs instrumental
   - Measure engagement difference
   - Balance trend vs brand fit

4. **CTA Testing**
   - "Save" vs "Comment" vs "Share"
   - Placement (end vs throughout)
   - Explicit vs implicit

### Iteration Cycle

```
Week 1: Baseline (post standard content)
Week 2: Test Hook A vs Hook B (2 videos each)
Week 3: Test Length variations
Week 4: Implement learnings, test CTA

Review monthly:
- Top 3 performers: What do they have in common?
- Bottom 3: What to avoid?
- Adjust content pillars accordingly
```
