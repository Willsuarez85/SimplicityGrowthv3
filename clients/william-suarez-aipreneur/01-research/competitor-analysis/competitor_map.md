## Direct Competitors (Spanish Language)

### 1. DotCSV / Carlos Santana Vega

| Attribute | Data |
|-----------|------|
| **Platform** | YouTube (primary), TikTok, Instagram |
| **Subscribers** | ~850,000 |
| **Location** | Madrid, Spain |
| **Content Type** | AI science, news, explainers |
| **Frequency** | 2-4 videos/month |
| **Average Length** | 15-25 minutes |

**Positioning:** Scientific AI divulgation, making complex ML concepts accessible

**Strengths:**
- Largest Spanish AI YouTube channel
- Strong academic credibility
- Consistent quality production
- Part of ML Hispano community
- Corporate partnerships (OpenExpo)

**Weaknesses:**
- Spain-focused (European Spanish)
- Academic angle, less practical application
- Not entrepreneur-focused
- No agent/automation building content
- Content can feel distant from business reality

**Differentiation from AIPreneur:**
DotCSV teaches *understanding* AI; AIPreneur teaches *building with* AI for business.

## Key Observations

### No Current Competitor Offers:
1. Spanish-language Claude Code education
2. MCP (Model Context Protocol) tutorials in Spanish
3. Anti-hype AI education for Hispanic entrepreneurs
4. Agent building focused on small business applications
5. Demonstrated systems from practitioner's own business

### AIPreneur's Unique Position:
The intersection of Spanish language + Claude Code + entrepreneur focus + anti-hype positioning is currently unoccupied.
