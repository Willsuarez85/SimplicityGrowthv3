## Executive Summary

Esta estrategia de Google Ads está diseñada para maximizar leads de alta calidad con $1,000/mes, enfocándose en:

1. **Emergency Services** - Mayor urgencia, mayor intent de conversión
2. **Handyman Services** - Volumen consistente, menor CPC
3. **Small Home Transformations** - Proyectos de mayor ticket

### Budget Allocation Recomendado

| Campaign | % Budget | Monthly $ | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Emergency Services | 40% | $400 | Highest conversion intent, premium pricing |
| Handyman General | 35% | $350 | Volume driver, consistent leads |
| Home Transformations | 25% | $250 | Higher ticket, longer sales cycle |

## Bidding Strategy Timeline

### Month 1: Learning Phase
- **Strategy:** Maximize Clicks
- **Goal:** Gather data, 50+ clicks minimum
- **CPC Cap:** $15 for emergency, $10 for handyman, $12 for transformations

### Month 2: Optimization Phase
- **Strategy:** Switch to Maximize Conversions
- **Requirement:** 15+ conversions
- **Action:** Pause underperforming keywords, double down on winners

### Month 3+: Efficiency Phase
- **Strategy:** Target CPA
- **Target:** $50-60 per lead
- **Action:** Automated bidding with manual oversight
