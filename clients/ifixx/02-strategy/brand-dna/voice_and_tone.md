## Voice Overview

Ifixx speaks like a trusted neighbor who happens to be a professional handyman. We're competent but not arrogant, friendly but not fake, direct but not cold. Our voice reassures homeowners that they made the right call.

---

## The Five Voice Traits

### 1. Reassuring
*"We've handled this before."*

**What it means:**
- Calm confidence in stressful situations
- Experience that creates peace of mind
- Solutions, not panic

**Do:**
- "Don't worry, we see this all the time."
- "We've fixed hundreds of these."
- "You called the right people."

**Don't:**
- "Oh wow, that's really bad."
- "I've never seen one this damaged."
- "This might be expensive..."

---

### 2. Direct
*"This is the issue. This is the fix. This is the cost."*

**What it means:**
- Clear, no-nonsense communication
- Honest about what's needed (and what's not)
- No beating around the bush

**Do:**
- "Here's what's wrong and what it takes to fix it."
- "This needs immediate attention."
- "Honestly, this can wait a few weeks."

**Don't:**
- "Well, there might be several issues..."
- "It could be this, or it could be that..."
- Vague language that creates confusion

---

### 3. Human
*"You talk directly with Jaime or Hamed."*

**What it means:**
- Real people, not corporate scripts
- Personal relationship over transaction
- We remember you

**Do:**
- "Hi, I'm Jaime â€” what's going on?"
- "We'll take care of you."
- "Call my cell if anything comes up."

**Don't:**
- "Thank you for calling Ifixx Services, please hold..."
- "Your call is important to us."
- Robotic, impersonal responses

---

### 4. Competent
*"Experienced, prepared, and dependable."*

**What it means:**
- We know what we're doing
- Confident without being cocky
- Professional standards

**Do:**
- "I've done this repair many times."
- "Let me show you exactly what I'm fixing."
- "This is standard procedure for this type of issue."

**Don't:**
- "Let me figure this out..."
- Hesitation or uncertainty
- Overpromising results

---

### 5. Local
*"We work in Charlotte homes every week."*

**What it means:**
- Part of the Charlotte community
- Know the local issues (weather, home styles)
- Invested in the neighborhood

**Do:**
- "We're just over in [neighborhood]."
- "A lot of homes in this area have this issue."
- "Carolina weather is tough on these."

**Don't:**
- Generic, could-be-anywhere language
- Unfamiliarity with Charlotte
- Corporate distance

## Tone Variations by Situation

### Emergency Call (Urgent/High Stress)
**Tone:** Calm, reassuring, action-oriented

| They Say | We Say |
|----------|--------|
| "My pipe burst!" | "Okay, first turn off the water main. I'll be there in 30 minutes. Stay calm â€” we've got you." |
| "The power went out and there's smoke!" | "If you see smoke, get everyone out safely first. I'm on my way. Call 911 if needed." |

**Key words:** Immediately, right away, don't worry, we're on it, stay safe

---

### Estimate/Quote (Evaluating)
**Tone:** Direct, honest, transparent

| They Say | We Say |
|----------|--------|
| "How much to fix this?" | "For this repair, you're looking at $X-$Y. I'll know exactly once I see it, but that's the typical range." |
| "Is this expensive?" | "Here's the honest breakdown: parts are $X, labor is $Y. No hidden fees." |

**Key words:** Here's what it costs, no surprises, typical range, honest assessment

---

### Follow-Up (Post-Service)
**Tone:** Warm, appreciative, available

| They Say | We Say |
|----------|--------|
| "Everything working great!" | "Glad to hear it! Call us anytime if you need anything else." |
| "I have a question about the repair." | "Of course â€” what's on your mind? Happy to explain." |

**Key words:** Anytime, happy to help, glad to hear, let us know

---

### Marketing/Social (Public Facing)
**Tone:** Confident, relatable, community-focused

**Do:**
- Share real before/after photos
- Feature Jaime and Hamed personally
- Celebrate Charlotte community
- Highlight fast response stories

**Don't:**
- Overhype or exaggerate
- Use stock photos or generic content
- Hard sell or pressure tactics
- Negative talk about competitors

## Language Guidelines

### Words We USE

| Category | Words |
|----------|-------|
| Speed | Fast, quick, same-day, right away, immediately |
| Trust | Honest, reliable, dependable, trustworthy, straightforward |
| Local | Charlotte, local, nearby, community, neighbors |
| Service | Fix, repair, solve, help, take care of |
| Relationship | Family, owner, personal, direct, you and us |

### Words We AVOID

| Category | Words |
|----------|-------|
| Corporate | Client, service representative, ticket, case number |
| Pressure | Limited time, act now, last chance, don't miss |
| Vague | Maybe, possibly, we'll see, it depends |
| Negative | Problem (use "issue"), broken (use "needs repair") |
| Pretentious | Premium, luxury, exclusive, high-end |
