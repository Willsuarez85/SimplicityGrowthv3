## Executive Summary

The Charlotte, NC handyman and emergency repair market represents a **viable and growing opportunity** for Ifixx. With Charlotte's population exceeding 920,000 (2024), the metro area experiencing 2.79% population growth (highest since 2014), and housing stock aging (median home age exceeds 40 years), demand for professional repair services continues to rise.

**Key Market Indicators:**
- U.S. Handyman Services industry size: **$355.3 billion** (2025)
- Charlotte market characterized by mix of national franchises and local independents
- Average hourly rates: **$40-80/hour** (Charlotte specific)
- Emergency service premium: **$50-150 additional** for after-hours
- Low regulatory barriers in Charlotte (no handyman license required as of 2015)

## Key Market Drivers

### 1. Demographic Shifts
- **Aging Population:** 83 million Americans will be 65+ by 2054
- **Aging-in-Place Trend:** Seniors prefer home modifications over assisted living
- **Millennial Homeownership:** Increasing with "Do It For Me" (DIFM) preference
- **Gen Z:** Lacks traditional trade skills, outsources repairs

### 2. Economic Factors
- **Aging Housing Stock:** Median U.S. home age exceeds 40 years
- **Renovation Spending:** Average American spends $5,635/year on home improvements
- **Home Decor Spending:** $1,598/year per household
- **High Mortgage Rates:** Homeowners staying put, investing in existing homes

### 3. Behavioral Changes
- **Hybrid Work:** More time at home = more noticed repairs
- **DIY Decline:** Baby Boomers aging out of capability
- **Convenience Expectations:** Consumers expect professional, on-demand service

### 4. Technology Integration
- **Smart Home Growth:** $174B (2025) to $250.6B (2029)
- **Household Smart Penetration:** 77% (2025), 92%+ projected (2029)
- **Digital Booking:** Consumers expect online scheduling and payment
