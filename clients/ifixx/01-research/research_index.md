## Research Summary

Comprehensive market research was conducted comparing two strategic niches for Ifixx:

1. **Kitchen & Bath Remodeling** - Higher revenue per job, but highly competitive
2. **Handyman & Emergency Services** - Faster ROI, more market gaps, better fit

**Outcome:** Handyman & Emergency Services selected as PRIMARY focus with viability score of **7.95** vs. 5.45 for K&B.

## Key Research Findings

### Market Opportunity

- **U.S. Handyman Market:** $355.3B
- **Charlotte Growth:** Strong (population growth driving demand)
- **Addressable Market:** All homeowners (vs. K&B's $15K+ budget segment)

### Competitive Landscape

| Factor | Finding |
|--------|---------|
| Established Competitors | 10+ but fragmented |
| Franchise Presence | Strong but beatable |
| Digital Competition | **WEAK - gaps everywhere** |
| Content Marketing Gaps | **Many gaps** |

### Financial Profile

| Metric | Handyman |
|--------|----------|
| Avg. Project Value | $200 - $2,000 |
| Profit Margin | 30-45% |
| Cash Flow Cycle | Days |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | $50-150 |
| Marketing Budget Needed | $1,500-3,000/mo |
| Time to First Revenue | Same week |

### Market Gaps Identified (7)

1. **Emergency 24/7 Service** - Almost no competition
2. **Subscription/Membership Models** - No competition
3. **Online Booking Experience** - Minimal competition
4. **Transparent Pricing** - No competition
5. **Property Management Specialist** - Limited competition
6. **Video Content Marketing** - Low-medium competition
7. **Nextdoor Dominance** - Low competition
