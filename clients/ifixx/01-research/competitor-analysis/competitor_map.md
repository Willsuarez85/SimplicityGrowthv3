| Metric | Data |
|--------|------|
| Years in Market | 25+ years (brand), 7+ years (South Charlotte location) |
| Employees | 6 (South Charlotte location) |
| Angi Rating | 3.7-4.9/5 (varies by location) |
| HomeAdvisor Rating | 4.1-4.2/5 |
| BBB Rating | A+ |
| Pricing Model | Hourly + materials markup + van stock charge |

**Positioning:**
- "One-stop solution for home repairs, maintenance, and improvements"
- Emphasizes fully licensed, insured, background-checked professionals
- Average 10+ years experience per technician

**Services:**
- Handyman, Window Installation, Carpentry, Gutter Cleaning
- Pressure Washing, Deck Building/Repair, Flooring
- Door Repair/Installation, Fence Services, Assembly

**Strengths:**
- National brand recognition
- Comprehensive training and systems
- Strong review presence

**Weaknesses:**
- Higher pricing (includes fuel charge, van stock charge ~$30 total)
- No free estimates
- Running to store for supplies is billable time
- Limited hours: M-F 8AM-5PM only

## Competitive Intelligence Summary

### What Market Leaders Do Consistently
1. Background checks and insurance prominently displayed
2. Strong Google/Yelp review presence
3. Clear service lists on websites
4. Years of experience highlighted
5. Customer testimonials with names

### Common Gaps Observed
1. Limited after-hours availability (most are M-F 8-5)
2. No transparent pricing online
3. Minimal social media/content marketing
4. Few subscription/membership offerings visible
5. Emergency handyman (vs. plumber/electrician) underserved
