## Executive Summary

The Charlotte Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling market presents a **viable opportunity** for Ifixx, a 5-year-old company with 5 employees. The market benefits from strong population growth (15.33% since 2020), rising home values (median $420K), and significant industry growth projections. However, competition is intense with established players dominating local search and reviews.

**Key Findings:**
- Market Size: North America bath remodeling alone at $67.8B (2023), projected to reach $88.4B by 2032
- Charlotte Population: 920,000+ residents, one of the fastest-growing metros in the US
- Average Project Values: Bathrooms $2,500-$120,000+, Kitchens $15,345-$160,000+
- Search Volume: "bathroom remodel" 259,750/month, "kitchen remodel" 148,417/month nationally
- Competition: 10+ established players with 20-30+ years experience, but room for specialized niches

**Viability Assessment:** VIABLE with differentiation strategy
