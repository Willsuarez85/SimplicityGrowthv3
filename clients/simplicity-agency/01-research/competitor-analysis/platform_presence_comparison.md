## Key Observations

### What Top Performers Do

1. **Clear Value Proposition**: Spanish+Media leads with specific performance metrics
2. **Niche Positioning**: Hispanic Market Advisors owns "Spanish SEO" space
3. **Certification Leverage**: BOLD uses MBE/WBE certifications strategically
4. **Scale Messaging**: Entravision leads with 3,000+ clients

### What's Missing in the Market

1. **Consistent, High-Quality Social Content**: Most competitors have gaps
2. **Video-First Approach**: No competitor leading with video content
3. **Educational Content Strategy**: Limited free educational resources
4. **Community Building**: No visible community/engagement strategies
5. **AI Education**: Competitors mention AI but don't educate about it
6. **Small Business Targeting**: Most content targets enterprise/mid-market
