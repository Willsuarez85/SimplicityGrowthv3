## Key Pattern Observations

### What Top Performers Use Consistently

1. **Brand-consistent visual identity** - Colors, fonts, style
2. **Professional photography** - Event, team, culture
3. **Cultural celebration content** - Heritage months, Latino pride
4. **Partner collaboration** - Co-branded content
5. **Metric-driven messaging** - Specific numbers

### What Appears Intentionally Avoided

1. **High-volume, low-quality posting** - Spanish+Media avoids this
2. **Hard-sell promotional content** - Top performers minimize
3. **Stock imagery over-reliance** - Better performers use original
4. **Complex jargon** - Accessible language preferred

### Format Evolution Indicators

1. **Video growing** - IGTV presence indicates video investment
2. **Carousel adoption** - Used for educational content
3. **Story integration** - Expected but not visible in data
4. **Reel opportunity** - Underutilized across market

## Content Strategy Insights

### Winning Patterns

1. **Quality over quantity** - Spanish+Media outperforms HMA
2. **Cultural connection** - Heritage content performs well
3. **Community involvement** - Partner content extends reach
4. **Human element** - Creator spotlights engage audiences
5. **Professional production** - Visual quality matters

### Losing Patterns

1. **Volume without strategy** - HMA's 584 posts, 85 followers
2. **Text-heavy graphics** - Low engagement
3. **Pure promotion** - Fails to engage
4. **Inconsistent branding** - Reduces recognition
5. **Stock imagery dependence** - Lacks authenticity
