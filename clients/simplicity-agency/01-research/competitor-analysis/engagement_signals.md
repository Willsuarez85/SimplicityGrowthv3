#### Hispanic Market Advisors (@hispanicmarketadvisors)

**What Appears to Generate Engagement:**
- AI and SEO tip content (educational)
- Hispanic influencer spotlights
- Industry news sharing

**What Appears to Fall Flat:**
- High volume is not translating to followers
- Service promotion posts
- Text-heavy graphics
- Repetitive themes

**Content Themes Observed:**
1. AI SEO updates
2. Translation service promotion
3. Legal marketing content
4. Influencer highlights
5. Localization tips

**Posting Consistency:**
- Very high frequency
- Daily or near-daily posting
- Quantity approach not working

**Critical Observation:**
584 posts with only 85 followers suggests a fundamental disconnect between content strategy and audience interest. The high output/low engagement pattern indicates:
- Content not resonating with target audience
- Possible algorithm suppression
- Wrong content format for platform
- Audience targeting issues

## Key Engagement Observations

### What Works in This Market

1. **Cultural pride content** resonates strongly
2. **Partner/community content** extends reach
3. **Human stories** generate emotional connection
4. **Educational content** attracts professionals
5. **Specific metrics** build credibility

### What Doesn't Work

1. **High-volume, low-quality posting** (HMA pattern)
2. **Pure promotional content** fails to engage
3. **Text-heavy graphics** underperform
4. **Corporate language** doesn't resonate
