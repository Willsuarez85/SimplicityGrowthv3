## HIGH-PRIORITY: Start This Week

### 1. Behind-the-Scenes Fresh Tortilla Production

**What's Working:**
- Process videos showing traditional nixtamalization and fresh tortilla making
- Time-lapse or real-time documentation of daily production
- "We make 500 before you wake up" time-based urgency hooks

**Evidence:**
- BTS content = top performer across all platforms (2026)
- Fresh tortilla differentiation = key competitive advantage
- 81.3% Hispanic digital video penetration (highest of all groups)

**La Unica Implementation:**
- Film 5am tortilla production (15-30 second clips)
- Show masa preparation → press → fresh stack
- Hook: "We make 500 tortillas before 7am every morning"
- Platform: TikTok + Instagram Reels (post daily or 3-5x/week)

**Expected Performance:**
- High completion rate (satisfying transformation)
- Strong saves (people want to remember La Unica)
- Profile visits from viewers seeking location

## CONTENT FORMATS WINNING RIGHT NOW

### Format 1: Time-Lapse Transformations (15-45 seconds)
- Masa → tortilla
- Raw dough → golden pan dulce
- Meat cut → seasoned carne asada
- Empty shelves → fully stocked (morning prep)

**Why:** Satisfying, high completion rate, shareable

### Format 2: POV "Come With Me" Tours (30-60 seconds)
- "Come with me to the panaderia at 6am"
- "Come shopping for authentic taco ingredients"
- "Come meet our butcher"

**Why:** Immersive, trending format, builds connection

### Format 3: Educational "How To" (30-60 seconds short, 8-12 min long)
- "How to pick the right cut for carne asada"
- "How to store fresh tortillas"
- "How to choose ripe avocados (produce tips)"

**Why:** High save rate, positions as authority, evergreen

### Format 4: Before/After Comparisons (30-45 seconds)
- Fresh tortilla vs store-bought (taste test)
- La Unica quality vs chain store
- Homemade vs La Unica pan dulce (tie = respect both)

**Why:** Clear value demonstration, drives comments/debate

### Format 5: Customer Reactions (15-30 seconds)
- First-time trying fresh tortilla
- Regular customer explaining why they drive 30 minutes
- "What do you love about La Unica?" quick interviews

**Why:** Social proof, authentic, relatable, trust-building

### Best Times to Post (Food/Grocery Industry)

| Platform | Best Days | Best Times | Why |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----|
| TikTok | Weekdays | 2-3 PM | Lunch scrolling, dinner planning |
| Instagram Reels | Thu-Fri | 2-3 PM | Highest food content engagement |
| YouTube | Tue-Thu | 11 AM-1 PM | Midweek lunch breaks |
