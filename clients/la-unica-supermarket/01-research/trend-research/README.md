## Key Findings Summary

### What's Working NOW (January 2026)

1. **Behind-the-Scenes Content** - Top performer across all platforms
2. **Pan Dulce Innovation** - Viral potential (19M+ views for Pan Pan Bakery)
3. **Fresh Tortilla Production** - Differentiator and trust-builder
4. **User-Generated Content** - 9.8x more impactful than influencer content
5. **Carniceria Expertise** - Educational content drives authority
6. **Cultural Storytelling** - Builds loyalty beyond transactions

### Platform Priorities for La Unica

| Platform | Priority | Why | Investment Level |
|----------|----------|-----|------------------|
| **Instagram Reels** | #1 | 73% Hispanic adult penetration | High |
| **TikTok** | #2 | Viral discovery, younger demographic | Medium-High |
| **YouTube** | #3 | SEO, long-form storytelling | Medium |

### Competitive Advantages

- **Daily Fresh Production:** Content goldmine (tortillas, pan dulce, carniceria prep)
- **Multi-Department Depth:** Full authentic experience (not just one category)
- **Cultural Heritage:** Family-owned, traditional methods, genuine story
- **Charlotte Market Gap:** Limited authentic Mexican grocery video content
