## Competitor Categories

### Direct Competitors
Same service category (Latino/Mexican supermarkets), same geographic market (Charlotte, NC)

### Indirect Competitors
Adjacent categories (international grocers, mainstream grocers with ethnic aisles), overlapping audience

### Reference Brands
Aspirational models from other markets demonstrating best practices

## Indirect Competitors

### Super G Mart Charlotte
- International grocery (not specifically Latino)
- Listed in "Top Latin Grocery" searches
- Broader Asian and international product mix

### Atlantic International Market
- Multi-ethnic grocery store
- Overlapping audience

### Mainstream Grocers (Kroger, Harris Teeter)
- Expanding "Mercado" and Hispanic sections
- Kroger launched Hispanic-inspired private brand "Mercado" (2023)
- Competitive on price but not on authenticity

## Key Observations

1. **Compare Foods is the clear digital leader** with nearly 15x the followers of nearest competitor
2. **Most competitors have weak Instagram presence** (<2K followers)
3. **All competitors emphasize fresh prepared foods** as differentiator
4. **Community integration** (events, partnerships) correlates with digital success
5. **"Authenticity" is universal claim** - proof points become differentiators
6. **E-commerce adoption is nascent** - Cardenas shows the path forward
