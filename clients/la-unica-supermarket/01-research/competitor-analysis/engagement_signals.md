## Platform-Specific Engagement Patterns

### Instagram Engagement Drivers

**What drives engagement in this market:**

1. **Reels/Video**: 36% more reach than static posts (industry data)
2. **Carousels**: 12% more engagement than single images
3. **Stories with polls/questions**: 5-8% response rate
4. **Location tags**: Local discovery benefit
5. **Hashtag strategy**: Mix of location + category + cultural

**Underutilized opportunities:**
- Live content (food prep, store tours)
- Collaboration posts with local businesses
- User-generated content curation
- Instagram Shopping for products

### Facebook Engagement Drivers

**What works for grocery retail:**

1. **Event promotion**: Highest organic reach
2. **Video content**: Algorithm preference
3. **Community groups**: Organic engagement hub
4. **Reviews/recommendations**: Trust building
5. **Weekly ad albums**: Functional engagement

**Talpa's Advanced Usage:**
- Facebook Pixel for retargeting
- Facebook Web Custom Audiences
- Indicates sophisticated digital marketing

## Comment Pattern Analysis

### Types of Comments Observed (Industry Pattern)

**High-Value Comments:**
- Product availability questions
- Location/hours inquiries
- Recipe requests
- Personal recommendations to friends (tagging)
- Nostalgia expressions ("reminds me of home")

**Engagement Indicators:**
- Question comments = high interest
- Tag comments = organic reach extension
- Emoji-only = passive engagement
- Save/bookmark = high intent

### Response Expectations

**Best Practices:**
- Bilingual responses
- Within 2-4 hours during business hours
- Personal, not canned responses
- Question-back engagement (extends conversation)

## Competitive Engagement Gaps

### Gap 1: TikTok Engagement

- No competitor has significant TikTok presence
- Platform demographic aligns with US-born Latino majority
- Food content performs well on TikTok
- Behind-the-scenes content is native to platform

### Gap 2: User-Generated Content

- No competitor visibly features customer content
- UGC drives 6x higher engagement than brand content
- Represents trust-building opportunity
- Photo opportunities in-store could generate UGC

### Gap 3: Interactive Content

- Polls, questions, quizzes not observed from local competitors
- Stories engagement is highest with interactive elements
- Recipe polls ("What should we make next?")
- Product preference questions

### Gap 4: Community Response

- Comment response practices unclear for most competitors
- Visible community management is differentiator
- Bilingual response capability is essential
