## Format Distribution (Estimated)

### Instagram Content Mix

**Compare Foods Charlotte** (based on 1,399 posts):
- Static images: ~60% (product shots, weekly ads)
- Carousels: ~20% (multi-product features, weekly ad pages)
- Reels/Video: ~15% (store tours, food prep)
- Stories: Active (promotions, time-sensitive offers)

**Wow Supermarket** (based on 324 posts):
- Static images: ~70% (products, store features)
- Carousels: ~15% (product groupings)
- Reels/Video: ~10% (food preparation)
- Stories: Unknown

**Reference Brand - Cardenas Markets** (based on 2,083 posts):
- Static images: ~40%
- Carousels: ~25%
- Reels/Video: ~30%
- Stories: Daily (promotions, flash sales)

**Industry Benchmark (per Buffer research):**
- Reels achieve 36% more reach than other post types
- Carousels achieve 12% more engagement than other post types
- Stories are most popular format for daily engagement

## Posting Cadence Patterns

### Weekly Rhythm

**Compare Foods (Market Leader):**
- Weekly ad drop: Mid-week (Wed/Thu)
- Product features: 2-3x/week
- Community content: 1-2x/week
- Estimated total: 4-6 posts/week

**Wow Supermarket:**
- Weekly ad: Variable
- Product features: 1-2x/week
- Event content: When applicable
- Estimated total: 2-3 posts/week

**Reference Brands:**
- Cardenas: 5-7 posts/week + daily Stories
- Talpa: 4-5 posts/week

### Seasonal Patterns

High-activity periods observed:
- **Mexican Independence (September 16)**: Heavy promotional activity
- **Dia de los Muertos (November 1-2)**: Cultural content spike
- **Christmas/Navidad**: Extended promotional period
- **Super Bowl**: Cross-cultural promotional opportunity
- **Easter/Semana Santa**: Traditional food features

## Content That Drives Engagement

### High-Engagement Content Types (Industry Research)

Based on Hispanic marketing research:

1. **Family-centric content**: 42% higher engagement when featuring multiple generations
2. **Cultural celebration content**: Spikes during heritage months and holidays
3. **Fresh food preparation**: Behind-the-counter content performs well
4. **Community event coverage**: Builds local connection
5. **Staff features**: Humanizes brand, builds trust

### Engagement Drivers Observed

**Compare Foods:**
- Charlotte FC partnership content
- Community event photos
- Weekly ad posts (practical value)

**Wow Supermarket:**
- Festival Latino event content
- Fresh food preparation videos
- Quality-focused messaging

## Content Gaps Identified

### Underutilized by Local Competitors

1. **User-generated content**: Customer photos/videos not prominently featured
2. **Recipe content**: Minimal how-to or recipe posts
3. **Staff stories**: Employee spotlights rare
4. **Educational content**: Ingredient spotlights, cooking tips absent
5. **Reels/short-form video**: Underutilized relative to potential
6. **TikTok content**: Nearly absent from market

### Overutilized/Saturated

1. **Price-focused weekly ads**: Universal approach, limited differentiation
2. **Generic product photos**: Similar style across competitors
3. **"Fresh" and "authentic" claims**: Universal positioning
