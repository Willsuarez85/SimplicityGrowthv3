## Brand Identity Summary (Observed)

### Stated Position
- "Todo lo tradicional de Mexico en un solo lugar" (All the traditional of Mexico in one place)
- Fresh meats, seafood, produce, and authentic Mexican products
- Full-service carniceria (butcher), panaderia (bakery), and tortilleria (tortilla shop)

### Market Position (Actual)
- Recognized as one of the most well-known Mexican and Latino supermarkets in Charlotte
- Ranked in Top 10 for Carniceria, Tortilleria, and Mexican Store categories on Yelp
- Multi-location operation (Independence Blvd + South Blvd)
- Serves Hispanic/Latino community seeking authentic Latin American products

### Value Proposition Elements (Documented)
1. Authenticity - Products from various Latin American countries
2. Fresh offerings - Meat counter, bakery, tortillas made in-house
3. One-stop shop - Grocery, money transfers, diner/restaurant
4. Cultural connection - Atmosphere reflecting Mexican roots
